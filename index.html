Jiaqin Wu

Data Scientist @ Center for Global Health Practice and Impact, Georgetown

I am a data scientist at CGHPI, Georgetown University. I am interested in using data science to analyze different kinds of policy issues, especially in public health and criminal justice policy fields. I have over 3 years of experience in using data science to analyze public policy issues. In the past, I worked on developing ML models to predict the treatment outcome for HIV patients in Haiti to help them keep in a good health outcome.

I obtained my master's degree in Data Science for Public Policy in 2024 from McCourt School for Public Policy, Georgetown University, where I worked on multiple research projects to understand how data science can be applied in public policy field. I developed data science skills in making interactive data visualizations and web applications.
