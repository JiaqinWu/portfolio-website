About

I am a data scientist at CGHPI, Georgetown University. I am interested in using data science to analyze different kinds of policy issues, especially in public health and criminal justice policy fields. I have over 10 years of experience in life sciences and healthcare. In the past, I worked on developing ML methods leveraging biomedical data including biological sequences, multi-omics, and longitudinal electronic health records (EHR), for drug discovery and modeling of human diseases.

I obtained my master’s degree in Data Science for Public Policy in 2024 from McCourt School for Public Policy, Georgetown University, where I worked on multiple research projects to understand how drugs work in biological systems using computational biology. I developed data science skills in making interactive data visualizations and web applications.
