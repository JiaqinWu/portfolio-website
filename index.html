Jiaqin Wu

Data Scientist @ Center for Global Health Practice and Impact, Georgetown University

I am a data scientist at CGHPI, Georgetown University. My interests lie in applying data science to tackle policy challenges, with a focus on public health and criminal justice. Over the past three years, I've worked on projects that use machine learning to analyze policy data. One of my projects involved building predictive models to help improve treatment outcomes for HIV patients in Haiti, supporting efforts to maintain positive health outcomes.

I got my master’s degree in Data Science for Public Policy in 2024 from the McCourt School of Public Policy at Georgetown University. During my studies, I worked on several research projects exploring how data science can address public policy issues. I also developed skills in creating interactive data visualizations and web applications to make data insights more accessible and actionable.
